## Missouri

### 🏛️ Official State & Federal Agencies

- [Missouri Veterans Commission (MVC)](https://mvc.dps.mo.gov/) — State agency that aids veterans, dependents, and families with benefits, veterans homes, cemeteries, and claims assistance.
- [Missouri Veterans Benefits & Resources Portal](https://veteranbenefits.mo.gov/) — One-stop portal for state and federal veteran benefits, resources, and VSO locator.
- [MO.gov Veterans Hub](https://www.mo.gov/veterans/) — Missouri's official gateway to employment, education, housing, crisis lines, and more.
- [VA St. Louis Regional Office](https://www.benefits.va.gov/stlouis/) — Administers compensation and Vocational Rehabilitation & Employment (VR&E) benefits for Missouri veterans.
- [Missouri Department of Mental Health – Veterans Resources](https://dmh.mo.gov/) — State mental health agency with veteran-specific programs and crisis services.
- [Missouri Military Resource Locator](https://myarmybenefits.us.army.mil/Benefit-Library/State/Territory-Benefits/Missouri) — Comprehensive Army benefits summary for Missouri service members and veterans.

### 📋 Benefits & Claims Assistance

- [MVC Veterans Service Officers (VSO) Locator](https://veteranbenefits.mo.gov/) — Free, trained VSOs who assist with compensation, pension, education, insurance, medical, and burial benefit claims.
- [Missouri Veterans Benefits Guide (PDF)](https://mvc.dps.mo.gov/docs/veterans-benefits-guide.pdf) — Printable guide to all Missouri state and federal veteran benefits.
- [Disabled American Veterans (DAV) Missouri](https://www.dav.org/) — Free professional assistance with VA claims, appeals, and benefits.
- [Paralyzed Veterans of America](https://pva.org/) — Specialized expertise for veterans with spinal cord injury or dysfunction, including benefits advocacy.
- [Missouri National Guard Family Assistance Centers](https://moguard.com/) — 12 FACs statewide providing support to Guard families during deployments and emergencies.

### 🧠 Mental Health & Crisis Support

- [Veterans Crisis Line](https://www.veteranscrisisline.net/) — Call **988, then press 1**. Free, confidential support 24/7 for veterans in crisis.
- [Access Crisis Intervention (ACI) Hotline](https://dmh.mo.gov/) — Free behavioral health crisis services available statewide; reduces unnecessary hospitalizations.
- [Missouri DMH Central Resource Hub](https://dmh.mo.gov/) — Single point of contact for non-emergency behavioral health guidance; returns calls within 48–72 hours.
- [Give an Hour](https://giveanhour.org/) — Volunteer mental health professionals offering free, confidential services to post-9/11 veterans and families.
- [K9s on the Front Line](https://www.k9sonthefrontline.org/) — Provides trained service dogs at no cost to veterans affected by PTSD and/or TBI.
- [Camp Hope – Farmington, MO](https://camphopemo.org/) — Expenses-paid retreat for veterans wounded in the War on Terror; located in Farmington, MO.
- [Hope Project STL](https://hopeprojectstl.org/) — St. Louis-area nonprofit using equine-assisted programs to support veterans, first responders, and families.
- [Valor Recovery Program – North Kansas City](https://valorportal.com/) — Trauma processing and stress management for active duty, veterans, and first responders.
- [PsychArmor Institute](https://psycharmor.org/) — Free online courses helping civilians and providers better support military members and veterans.

### 🏠 Housing & Homelessness Prevention

- [HUD-VASH Program](https://www.hud.gov/program_offices/public_indian_housing/programs/hcv/vash) — Combines HUD housing vouchers with VA case management and clinical services for homeless veterans.
- [Missouri Housing Development Commission](https://www.mhdc.com/) — Affordable housing resources for all Missourians, including veterans.
- [Welcome Home – Columbia, MO](https://www.welcomehomemo.org/) — Mid-Missouri's only veteran-centered emergency shelter; provides meals, housing, case management, and wraparound services at no cost.
- [Veterans Community Project](https://www.veteranscommunityproject.org/) — Builds communities of tiny homes in Kansas City to provide housing stability for homeless veterans.
- [St. Michael's Veterans Center (SMVC)](https://stmichaelsveteranscenter.org/) — Support for veterans with employment, physical and mental health, trauma, addiction, and long-term housing stability.
- [MOVE: Missouri Veterans Endeavor – St. John, MO](https://www.move-stl.org/) — Long-term supportive housing and services for veterans and families in the St. Louis area.
- [Salvation Army SSVF Program](https://www.salvationarmyusa.org/) — Supportive Services for Veteran Families (SSVF): helps low-income veterans obtain and maintain stable housing.
- [Mid-Missouri Fisher House](https://www.fisherhouse.org/) — Free temporary lodging for families of veterans receiving hospital care.

### 🎓 Education

- [Missouri Returning Heroes Education Act](https://dhewd.mo.gov/) — Caps tuition at $50/credit hour at public Missouri colleges for eligible combat veterans (post-9/11).
- [War Veterans' Survivor Grant Program](https://dhewd.mo.gov/) — Annual grants for children (under 25) and spouses of veterans killed or severely disabled in combat since 9/11.
- [University of Missouri Veterans Center](https://veteranscenter.missouri.edu/) — Resources, advising, and community for student veterans at Mizzou.
- [Missouri "Operation Recognition" Honorary Diplomas](https://dese.mo.gov/) — Honorary high school diplomas for veterans who left school to serve during wartime.
- [Missouri Purple Star School Program](https://dese.mo.gov/) — Recognizes military-friendly schools with dedicated liaisons and resources for military families.

### 💼 Employment

- [Show-Me Heroes Program](https://showmeheroes.mo.gov/) — 50% wage reimbursement to employers who hire and train qualifying veterans; honorable discharge required.
- [Employer Support of the Guard and Reserve (ESGR)](https://www.esgr.mil/) — Resolves employer conflicts for Reserve Component service members; workplace support and education.
- [Feds Hire Vets](https://www.fedshirevets.gov/) — Federal employment resources and veteran hiring preference information.
- [Missouri Veterans Employment Preference](https://oa.mo.gov/) — Veterans receive 5-point (10-point for service-connected disabled) preference in Missouri state employment testing.
- [VA Vocational Rehabilitation & Employment (VR&E)](https://www.va.gov/careers-employment/vocational-rehabilitation/) — Job training, education, and employment services for veterans with service-connected disabilities.

### 💰 Financial Assistance

- [Missouri Military Family Relief Fund (MMFRF)](https://mvc.dps.mo.gov/) — Financial assistance for qualified Missouri military members and their families.
- [Missouri Property Tax Credit (Circuit Breaker)](https://dor.mo.gov/) — Credit up to $1,100 for homeowners or $750 for renters who are 100% service-connected disabled veterans.
- [FISH – Greater Kansas City](https://fishkc.org/) — Emergency financial assistance for service members, veterans, and families in the KC metro area.
- [Semper Fi & America's Fund](https://semperfifund.org/) — Financial assistance and lifetime support for combat-wounded and critically ill veterans.
- [Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program (LIHEAP)](https://dss.mo.gov/fsd/liheap.htm) — Helps veterans and families pay home energy bills.

### ⚖️ Legal Services

- [University of Missouri School of Law Veterans Clinic](https://law.missouri.edu/clinics/) — Free legal assistance for veterans, including VA compensation claims and discharge upgrade applications.
- [National Veterans Legal Services Program (NVLSP)](https://www.nvlsp.org/) — Free representation for veterans with less-than-honorable discharges and PTSD/TBI-related appeals.

### 🏥 Health Care Facilities

- [VA Medical Center St. Louis – John Cochran Division](https://www.va.gov/st-louis-health-care/) — 915 N. Grand Blvd., St. Louis, MO 63106
- [VA Medical Center St. Louis – Jefferson Barracks Division](https://www.va.gov/st-louis-health-care/) — 1 Jefferson Barracks Dr., St. Louis, MO 63125
- [Vet Center St. Louis](https://www.va.gov/find-locations/) — 287 N. Lindbergh Blvd., Creve Coeur; readjustment counseling, job info, benefits, and more. Additional locations statewide.
- [Missouri VA Facilities Locator](https://www.va.gov/find-locations/) — Find VA hospitals, clinics, and Vet Centers throughout Missouri.

### 🤝 Community & Service Organizations

- [Veterans of Foreign Wars (VFW) – Missouri](https://movfw.org/) — Post directory and statewide advocacy, service, and camaraderie for combat veterans.
- [The Mission Continues – St. Louis](https://missioncontinues.org/) — Engages veterans in community service platoons across St. Louis and nationwide.
- [Wounded Warrior Project](https://www.woundedwarriorproject.org/) — Mental health, career counseling, and long-term rehabilitation programs for wounded post-9/11 veterans.
- [Jefferson Barracks National Cemetery](https://www.cem.va.gov/cems/nchp/jeffersonbarracks.asp) — One of the nation's oldest national cemeteries; burial place for veterans of all wars.
- [Missouri State Veterans Cemeteries](https://mvc.dps.mo.gov/cemetery.php) — Five state cemeteries (Springfield, Higginsville, Bloomfield, Ft. Leonard Wood, Jacksonville) with no-cost interment for eligible veterans.
